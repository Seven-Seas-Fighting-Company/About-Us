Founded Locally, In San Francisco
Founded in San Francisco by two passionate martial artists, Seven Seas Fighting Company began with a simple question during a weekend sparring session in the park: "What if martial arts gear didn't stink?" Driven by that idea, we pooled our resources and set out to create antibacterial gear.
Mission
To equip every martial artist with advanced anti-microbial gear, defeating odors and increasing sanitary standards.
Eco Friendly Manufacturing
We believe that raising the bar in the martial arts gear market goes hand in hand with environmental consciousness. All byproducts of our infusion process are converted into environmentally friendly fertilizers.
