Founded Locally, in San Francisco
The Seven Seas Fighting Company was founded by two broke dudes in San Francisco. During a weekend sparring session in the park we found ourselves wondering, “Hey, what if martial arts equipment didn’t stink?”. We then scraped together funds and made our vision into a reality.
Mission
To equip every martial artist with advanced anti-microbial gear, defeating odors and increasing sanitary standards.
Eco Friendly Manufacturing
We believe that raising the bar in the martial arts gear market goes hand in hand with environmental consciousness. All byproducts of our infusion process are converted into environmentally friendly fertilizers.
Looking to the Future
Our first product, antibacterial handwraps, are out now, selling direct to consumers in San Francisco. The nationwide launch is coming soon on amazon.com. But innovation doesn't stop with hand wraps. We’re gearing up to release antibacterial boxing gloves in a matter of months, as well as shin guards, jockstraps, headbands and more within a year.
